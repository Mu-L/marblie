THANK YOU FOR PLAYING MARBLIE!

Marblie is a playful physics sandbox.

Whether you're designing elaborate courses, tinkering with gravity, or just watching marbles swirl down a funnel for the hundredth time, this simulator is meant to spark joy and curiosity.

Build, test, and watch marbles race through your own custom tracks. It’s a toy, all about creativity.

This project started as a fun experiment — a mix of physics, design, and childhood nostalgia. Now it’s a toy for anyone who loves rolling things down ramps and seeing what happens.
